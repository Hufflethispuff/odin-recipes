Omelette

This recipe is for those busy bodies on the go. Don't have time to cook a big breakfast? Make yourself this quick and tasty omelette. Not only will it no dissapoint, but you will be out the door and off to whatever adventure this new day has in store for you.

Here is what you'll need:

  • 2 eggs
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • 1/4 of a tomato
  • 1/4 of a green pepper

Steps:

  1. Chop tomato and pepper then set it to the side.
  2. Whisk eggs, a dash of milk, and a pinch of salt in a mixing bowl. Any bowl will do, but please not your mothers china. You drop that and WE are both dead.
  3. Heat up a lightly oiled pan and pour in the egg mix.
  4. After one minute drop the chopped vegetables on one side of the omelette and flip the other over the vegetbale.
  5. Flip the omelette and cook for one minute. Serve Hot.
