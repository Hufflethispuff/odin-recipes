CREPES

Try this simple yet delicious recipe for crepes! All it takes is just 15 minutes and voila! You have yourself a stack of delicate crepes ready for the taking. The only reccomendation I have is to make sure everyone is out of the house. If not, you might actually have to share them. Trust me, you won't want to share these.

Let's get started! First you will need:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

    • Steps:

    1. Mix flour,eggs, and salt.
    2. Gradually add milk as you mix it in. Be sure to get rid of all the clumps!
    3. Heat a lightly oiled pan and add 1/4 cup of crepe mix. Tilt pan in circulater until the surface of the pan is covered in crepe mix.
    4. Cook crepe for about one minute, flip and cook other side for another minute.
    5. Serve on plate, cover it with whatver you like! Jam, syrup, powdered sugar, whatever you please! and enjoy!
